How do they taste? How to they feel?
Both Palitaw are quite delicious;

The Cheese palitaw has a great mix of flavors, the sweetness of the cheese, the milky flavor of the shredded coconut, and the brown sugar's toffee-like flavor all mix together in the palitaw. The dough itself has no flavor.

The Honey Sesame palitaw has a slight earthy flavor, and a mildly nutty flavor, The dough itself also has no flavor.

Both Palitaw are quite sticky and very soft by themselves, with their innovations, The Cheese palitaw has a rough texture due to the shredded coconut and the brown sugar while the Honey Sesame palitaw has a smooth and and slightly rough texture due to the honey and sesame seeds.
