Making these palitaw is quite simple, you can easily make some right at home. In this palitaw you will need the following ingredients;
Cheese Palitaw Honey Sesame Palitaw
Glutinous Rice Flour Glutinous Rice Flour
Water Water
Cheese(Any type) Honey
Shredded coconut Sesame Seeds
Brown Sugar
    Cheese palitaw process
  1. In a pan, or a pot, add water and start boiling it
  2. In a bowl, add 1 cup of glutinous rice.
  3. Add water little by little and mix until it has the consistency of clay
  4. Take a small portion and form a disc.
  5. Add the cheese onto the middle and then mold the palitaw into a ball(Make as many as you want!)
  6. Drop the balls into the boiling water and wait
  7. Once it floats to the top, pick up the balls and place onto a plate
  8. On a seperate plate, add the shredded coconut and some brown sugar.
  9. Take the palitaw and cover it into the shredded coconut and brown sugar
  10. Place onto proper plating and eat!
